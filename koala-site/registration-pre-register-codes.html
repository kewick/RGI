Registration Codes

Some organizations have provided users with a Pre-Registration Code for administrative purposes which must be entered during registration. If your organization provided you a code, enter it in the box provided and click Continue. Pre-Registration Codes can NOT be entered after this point. If you do not have a Pre-Registration Code, click the button "I am not using a Pre-Registration Code". If you believe you are supposed to have a code but do not have it available now, click the Logout button and contact your organization. Once you have been provided with the code, log back in to the program with the Username and Password you have created. The system will remember you left at the Pre-Registration Code page and you will be requested to enter the code.

Please note the Helpdesk for this program does not have access to codes and cannot provide assistance in this regard. You must contact your organization to access these codes.
